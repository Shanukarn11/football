What is ikf?

IKF is a non-profit initiative working with a single point objective to make football a viable career option for Indian kids.

IKF has created a digital platform which on its one end has kids who wants to become a professional football player and on the other end has opportunities from across the world in the form of clubs, agents, and scouts.

SOURCE

Arrange events and create a network of like minded individuals/organizations to get kids to play.

IDENTIFY

Spot exciting talent from across India via events and partnerships

PLATFORM

Provide kids with a global platform to showcase their talent and provide a fair chance to them to excel.
