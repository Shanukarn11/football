Frequently Asked Questions

Who can register for the City Trials? +
Boys and girls aged 10‑17, with a valid government ID and medical clearance.
What is the registration fee? +
INR 295 (250+45 GST ), one‑time processing fee for City Trials; no fees for girls; Zonal and National rounds are free´
What if my City isn’t on the list? +
We add new locations every year. Follow our Instagram for updates.
Does selection guarantee a club contract? +
Selection earns exposure. Final signings are decided solely by the clubs.
Is accommodation provided for National and Zonal Finals? +
IKF can share discounted options on request but does not cover costs directly.
