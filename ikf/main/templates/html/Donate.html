Why Support India Khelo Football

India Khelo Football (IKF) is a non-profit movement working to build the grassroots football ecosystem of India. Through structured trials, scouting partnerships, coaching workshops, and player development programs, we open doors for young athletes — girls and boys — across the country who would otherwise have no pathway into the sport.

Every contribution directly fuels a project: equipping a player, running a trial, training a coach, or sending a child to a national-level showcase. Your support keeps these opportunities alive.
