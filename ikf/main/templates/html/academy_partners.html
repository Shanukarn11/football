Academy Partner

India Khelo Football (IKF) recognizes football academies as the first builders of a player's dream. Our Academy Partners are not just feeders into trials—they are strategic collaborators who work closely with IKF's Regional Engagement Partners (REPs) to ensure talent development, fair access, and long-term player growth.

This partnership is designed to be clear, transparent, and mutually empowering, where academies retain their identity while gaining access to national opportunities.

The IKF Academy Partnership Model

IKF follows a collaborative, on-ground partnership model where Academy Partners and REPs work in sync—each with defined roles, shared objectives, and aligned outcomes.

How the Ecosystem Works

Academy Partner ↔ REP ↔ IKF Trials

  • Academy Partners focus on training, discipline, and day-to-day player development
  • REPs act as the local bridge—coordinating trials, communication, and compliance
  • IKF provides the national platform, structured trials, and professional scouting access

This ensures clarity, fairness, and seamless execution across regions.

Role of Academy Partners

As an IKF Academy Partner, your academy plays a central role in identifying, preparing, and supporting players.

Academy Partners:

  • Nominate and recommend players for IKF Trials through the assigned REP
  • Prepare players according to IKF trial standards and age criteria
  • Support ethical, merit-based selection—free from favoritism
  • Act as long-term development centers for identified players
  • Align with IKF values of inclusion, transparency, and opportunity

Role of REPs (Regional Engagement Partners)

REPs ensure smooth coordination and fairness on the ground by:

  • Acting as the official IKF point of contact for academies
  • Facilitating academy access to IKF Trials and trial information
  • Coordinating logistics, timelines, and documentation
  • Ensuring transparent communication between academies and IKF
  • Supporting academies in aligning with IKF processes

This structure removes ambiguity and ensures equal access for all partner academies.

What Academy Partners Gain

IKF Academy Partners are valued contributors to the ecosystem and benefit from:

Access & Opportunity
  • Direct access to IKF State, Zonal, and National Trials via REPs
  • Exposure to ISL, I-League, and professional club scouts
  • A clear and recognized pathway from academy to national platform
Recognition & Visibility
  • Featured as an Official IKF Academy Partner
  • Visibility across IKF digital platforms and events
  • Opportunity to showcase academy success stories
Development & Support
  • Alignment with IKF Pathway 360 (life skills, education, mental conditioning)
  • Exposure to national best practices in talent identification
  • Long-term engagement beyond one-off trials

A Transparent & Merit-Based Pathway

IKF maintains a non-exclusive, merit-first approach:

  • Selection is based solely on player performance
  • No guaranteed selections—only fair opportunity
  • Clear eligibility, trial processes, and evaluation criteria
  • Open communication with academies via REPs

This ensures trust, credibility, and sustainability for all partners.

Why Academies Choose IKF

  • Respect for academy identity and autonomy
  • No bypassing—engagement happens through REPs
  • Nationwide reach without losing local control
  • Focus on long-term player journeys, not one-day outcomes
  • Trusted national platform with proven scouting credibility

Become an IKF Academy Partner

If your academy is committed to ethical development, player-first values, and structured opportunity, IKF welcomes you into its growing Academy Partner network.
