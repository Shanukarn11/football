A Vision Beyond the Pitch

Field of Dreams is a powerful social impact initiative by IKF Pathway 360 that supports aspiring athletes on and off the field. Our approach blends professional football development with critical life skills and modern literacy, enabling young players to survive challenges, navigate life, and thrive in any environment.

We aim to build resilient, skilled, and emotionally intelligent footballers through a holistic development framework.

Your Support, Their Dream

Every child deserves a chance. IKF's Field of Dreams categorizes player pathways to tailor their development journey and connect them with sponsors, mentors, and experts who can amplify their growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Field Of Dreams campaign?+

Field Of Dreams is a social impact initiative by IKF Pathway 360 that combines football development with life skills, education, and emotional well-being.

Who benefits from this campaign?+

Under-resourced footballers of all ages, including girls and injured players—each matched with the right pathway and support.

What are the program categories?+
  • Future Champions
  • Books & Boots
  • Maidaan Ki Rani
How can I support?+

Sponsor a player, adopt a program, or donate to training, healthcare, or education — every contribution counts.

Is this only for football players?+

Yes, but the program develops footballers beyond the game—ready for life, careers, and leadership.
