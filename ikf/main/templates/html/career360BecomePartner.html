Who Can Partner

We welcome individuals and groups offering support in:

  • Football coaching (all levels)
  • Sports psychology and mental wellness
  • Physiotherapy and rehabilitation
  • Academic mentoring and career guidance
  • Nutrition and fitness training
  • Talent scouting and sports analysis
  • Football-focused NGOs and educational institutes

If your work empowers young footballers, we want you on board.
