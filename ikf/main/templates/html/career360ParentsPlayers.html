Parent SOP

Clarity for parents. Pathways for players.

IKF Parent SOP is a simple, parent-first framework for supporting your child's football journey under IKF Pathway 360. It sets clear expectations, structure, and roles—so families can make confident, balanced decisions.

In short: guidelines that help you champion both football and education, with zero pressure and full transparency.

Purpose of IKF Parent SOP

  • Sport is treated as a platform for growth, identity, and resilience — not a distraction.
  • Helps parents understand the process and structure of IKF programs.
  • Clarifies the role parents play in supporting both sporting and personal development.
  • Maintains discipline, transparency, and fairness across all IKF activities.
  • Promotes a positive, constructive environment for players and families.
