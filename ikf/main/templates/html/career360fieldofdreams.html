A Vision Beyond the Pitch

Field of Dreams is a powerful social impact initiative by IKF Pathway 360 that supports aspiring athletes on and off the field. Our approach blends professional football development with critical life skills and modern literacy, enabling young players to survive challenges, navigate life, and thrive in any environment.

Field Of Dreams — Your Support, Their Dream

Every child deserves a chance. IKF's Field of Dreams categorizes player pathways to tailor their development journey and connect them with sponsors, mentors, and experts who can amplify their growth.
