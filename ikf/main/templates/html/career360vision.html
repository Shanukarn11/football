What is IKF Pathway 360?

IKF Pathway 360 is not just a program. It is a holistic ecosystem designed to nurture, guide, and sustain football journeys in India.

At India Khelo Football (IKF), we believe talent alone is not enough. What truly builds a professional footballer or a lifelong contributor to the sport is the right mix of opportunity, guidance, environment, and support systems.

IKF Pathway 360 is our answer to that need.
