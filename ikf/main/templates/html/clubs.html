Our Trusted Club & Academy Network

A year-round talent pathway linking India's clubs, academies, and players.

We partner with top-tier Indian clubs and football academies to create a robust talent pipeline and professional development ecosystem. From grassroots to top divisions, IKF connects the right players to the right environment through structured scouting and transparent pathways.

Who We Partner With

Associate Clubs

Collaborating with established clubs across all age groups and formats—from grassroots to professional level.

Associate Academies

How We Partner with Clubs & Academies

By joining IKF's network, clubs and academies unlock powerful benefits and streamlined access to talent and infrastructure:

Invitation to National Finals

Showcase event of 250+ top players from 125+ cities and villages across India at an invite-only event.

Scouting Partner

Access to IKF Trials across the year, from city rounds to national finals, for partner clubs & academies.

Scouting Alliance

We benchmark, analyse requirements, and deploy scouts to create a robust talent pipeline tailored to each club.

Official Scouting Partner

IKF has joined hands with organisations and clubs to provide a year-round talent pipeline so that the right talent connects to the right opportunities.
