desportz - IKF Centre of Excellence (IKF-CoE) Program

India’s first CoE was launched with desportz to provide an overall ecosystem for a scout to undergo Level 1 and 2 IKF Scouting certification at desportz Vadodara Centre where potential scouts will get hands-on experience to become IKF Certified Scout. For the first time in India IKF along with desportz is launching an onsite Instructor led course where you will learn Scouting via classroom session and on site IKF Vadodara Trials. Apart from that IKF has partnered with desportz to provide 11 months PGP certification in Sports Marketing and 3 month certification program in sports marketing.

IKF Level 1 and 2 Scouting Certification

Indian football is at a very interesting juncture, where we are seeing corporates and foreign clubs lining up to have a stake in its development. However, at the end of the day it is the talent which is going to take Indian Football ahead; not just talent – “Right Talent”. This right talent has to be identified at the right time and for that IKF is developing a strong Grassroots Scouting Ecosystem in India. IKF has built a platform from scratch that helps the ecosystem identify the top talent of our nation. IKF has built the following blocks to create this ecosystem in India:

Preface
