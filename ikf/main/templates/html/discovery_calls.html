PFSA Level 1 — International Basics

Learn from the world's leading scouting body.

About the Course

PFSA (Professional Football Scouts Association, UK) Level 1 is a 4-hour, self-paced online course trusted by scouts worldwide. As PFSA's India partner, IKF makes this international foundation accessible to Indian aspirants.

What You'll Learn

  • Fundamentals of talent ID in a global context
  • Core scouting principles and evaluation techniques
  • How to draft basic scouting reports

Who It's For

Beginners who want to gain global exposure to scouting principles.

Outcome

  • Internationally recognized credential
  • A strong entry point before deep-diving into India-specific scouting with IKF

IKF-CFSA Level 1 — Indian Context Foundation

The first step to becoming a scout in India.

About the Course

Designed by IKF exclusively for Indian conditions, Level 1 provides the theoretical foundation for football scouting in grassroots and professional environments.

What You'll Learn

  • Scouting lexicon & fundamentals
  • Talent discernment using the 4-Corner Model (Physical, Technical, Tactical, Psychosocial)
  • Spotting techniques & report writing

Who It's For

  • Aspirants who want to enter India's scouting ecosystem
  • Anyone looking to build a solid theoretical base before moving to applied scouting

Outcome

  • IKF Scout Badge
  • Listed on the IKF Scout Map
  • Eligible for trial/match scouting assignments
  • Mandatory entry point for IKF-CFSA Level 2

IKF-CFSA Level 2 — Elite Applied Certification

From learning scouting to shaping the game.

About the Course

Level 2 is where theory meets practice. In this 15-day intensive, scouts move beyond the basics to decode positional intelligence, apply advanced KPIs, and prove their skills through video and live scouting exams.

What You'll Learn

  • Positional scouting for Goalkeepers, Defenders, Midfielders, and Attackers
  • Role-specific traits in modern tactical systems (false 9, inverted FB, box-to-box CM, etc.)
  • Formation analysis, transitions, and psychosocial profiling
  • Real match case studies + hands-on assignments

Assessments

  • Exam 1 (Video): Pro match analysis & Mi-Tas reports (1 player per position line)
  • Exam 2 (Live): On-ground scouting during IKF Zonal or Finals

Who It's For & Outcome

  • Exclusively for IKF-CFSA Level 1 certified scouts
  • Recognition as part of an elite cohort of Indian scouts
  • Recruitment opportunities with I-League & ISL clubs
  • High respect in the scouting ecosystem
