Democratizing
the Dream

India Khelo Football is democratizing football by removing systemic barriers and giving access to every stakeholder—players, scouts, clubs, schools, parents, and CSR partners—regardless of geography, gender, or income.

For too long, pursuing football in India meant chasing privilege—right city, right academy, right contacts. IKF is changing that.

What Does It Mean to Democratize Football?

Open Trials Across India

  • Conducted in 125+ cities, towns, and underserved areas
  • Free for girls, nominal for boys
  • No barriers of region or background
  • Completely merit-based, not pay-to-play

Merit Over Money

  • Evaluations based on 25+ football performance metrics
  • No pay-for-selection, no hidden entries
  • Purely performance-driven, not connection-driven

Free Platform. Not Commercial.

  • IKF doesn't monetize players or events
  • No commission from scouts, clubs, or academies
  • Operates as a neutral bridge, not a biased stakeholder

Going Beyond the Pitch

Scouting Certification for Everyone

  • Anyone can become a certified scout—no background limits
  • Learn modern techniques: video analysis, data evaluation
  • Build a career by contributing meaningfully to the ecosystem

Connecting the Entire Ecosystem

IKF unites all forces shaping football:

  • Academies → Clubs
  • Players → Career Pathways
  • Parents → Mentors
  • CSR Funders → Impact Stories

IKF Pathway 360

A complete 360° support system for every stakeholder:

  • Players get trials, exposure & career guidance
  • Scouts get certification & placement support
  • Clubs & academies get access to verified talent
  • CSR partners get measurable social impact

This builds a truly collaborative, inclusive football ecosystem.

Real Impact. Real Futures.

  • Players from remote towns have earned ISL, I-League, European trials & U.S. scholarships.
  • Parents with no sports background get career planning through Pathway 360.
  • Certified volunteer scouts now work with ISL clubs.
  • Girls from tier-3 cities shine on a national platform—for free.
