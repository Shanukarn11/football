Why It Matters

The Talent
Is There.
The System Isn't.

India has millions of footballers with the skill and hunger to compete at the highest levels. What it lacks is a funded, structured system that finds them — regardless of geography, gender, or income. That gap is what your donation closes.

  • Girls who stop playing because no one funds their participation.
  • Players from small cities with no access to scouts or structured trials.
  • Injured players who cannot afford treatment and lose years of development.
  • Talented kids who have to choose between books and boots.
Your donation does not build stadiums. It builds pathways.

♀ Anaya's Goal & Naari Shakti

The Highest-Impact Gift You Can Give: A Girl's Trial.

Girls' football in India is underfunded, underscoured, and undervalued. IKF is changing that — but it takes donors to keep the door open. Anaya's Goal and Naari Shakti fund every dimension of girls' participation, from the first trial to the national stage.

Learn About Project IKF Naari Shakti

Free trials for every girl

Your donation to Anaya's Goal directly funds free registration for girls — meaning zero financial barrier between a girl and her first IKF trial.

Kit, travel & logistics

For girls from underserved regions, travel and kit are often bigger barriers than registration. Naari Shakti funds these directly so geography is never an excuse.

A dedicated, visible pathway

Every girl who participates in IKF becomes part of a permanent, growing record of female footballers in India. Visibility that does not exist anywhere else.

Donor Questions, Answered

Everything You Need to Know Before You Give

Direct answers for donors. No vague promises. No jargon.

Where does my donation to IKF go?

Directly to field operations — funding trials, girls' free participation, player support initiatives, and the infrastructure needed to run transparent evaluations across India. You can choose which initiative receives your donation.

Can I donate to a specific initiative?

Yes. Choose from Anaya's Goal, Future Champions, Naari Shakti, Books & Boots, Heal to Play, IKF 360 Field of Dreams, i-Care by Invictus, or Support IKF Trials — your donation goes exactly where you direct it.

How do I donate to IKF?

Online via Razorpay, by UPI using the IKF QR code, or by direct bank transfer. Visit indiakhelofootball.com/donate for all payment options with full receipt documentation.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

IKF is a not-for-profit initiative. Visit the donate page or contact IKF directly for current tax exemption status and the documentation needed for your records.

What is the minimum donation?

There is no minimum. Every rupee contributes to building a fair football ecosystem. A small recurring contribution often creates more sustained impact than a single large gift.

What is Anaya's Goal?

Anaya's Goal is IKF's dedicated girls' football fund — covering free trials, kit, and travel for girls from underserved regions. It is one of the highest-impact donations you can make to Indian football.
