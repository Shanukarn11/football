Player Questions, Answered

Everything You Need to Know Before You Register

Clear, direct answers for players and families. No jargon. No fine print.

What is IKF for football players?

IKF (India Khelo Football) is a not-for-profit platform that connects grassroots players to structured trials, scout visibility, and performance-based pathways to professional clubs — with zero room for agents or shortcuts.

How can I get scouted through IKF?

Register for your nearest city trial, play in front of scouts, get shortlisted for zonal rounds, advance to national finals, and earn club-facing exposure. Every step is based purely on performance.

Does IKF guarantee selection into a club?

No. IKF creates the platform and the opportunity. All final selection decisions are made by clubs based on performance and merit. IKF does not sell selection or promise club entry.

Who can participate in IKF trials?

Boys and girls across India can participate — from first-time trial players to aspiring professionals. IKF especially focuses on players from underrepresented cities and regions.

Is there a participation fee for IKF trials?

IKF participation for girls is free. For boys, a nominal registration fee may apply. IKF never charges for selection or club placement — any such request is fraud.

What happens after the trial if I'm not selected?

You become part of the IKF ecosystem with an honest benchmark of your current level. You can return for future editions, access IKF resources, and continue your development — non-selection is not the end.
