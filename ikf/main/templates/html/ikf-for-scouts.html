Scout Questions, Answered

Everything You Need to Know Before You Apply

Direct answers for anyone serious about scouting with IKF.

How do I become an IKF certified scout?

Book a discovery call through the IKF Scouting Certification page. You will be guided through the programme, eligibility, and next steps. Certification covers evaluation methodology, trial protocols, and assessment standards.

What is Scouting as a Service?

It allows IKF-certified scouts to offer structured talent evaluation services independently — to clubs, academies, or schools — using the IKF framework as the foundation of their service.

What are IKF Session Plans for scouts?

Structured, ready-to-deploy evaluation session plans that let you run professional scouting sessions anywhere — schools, clubs, academies — bringing the IKF standard to every setting.

Do I need prior scouting experience?

Not necessarily. IKF's certification programme is designed to train scouts at different experience levels. A background in football — as a player, coach, or observer — is helpful but not always required.

Can I scout independently and still use the IKF framework?

Yes. Through Scouting as a Service, certified IKF scouts can operate independently while using the IKF methodology — giving their independent evaluations the credibility of the IKF system.

What is the IKF Scout Network?

A national community of certified IKF scouts who collaborate, share resources, and support each other — collectively raising the standard of talent evaluation across India.
