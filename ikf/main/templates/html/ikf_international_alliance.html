Elevating Indian Football to Global Heights

At India Khelo Football (IKF), we are committed to transforming Indian football into a globally recognized sport. Through strategic international partnerships and structured pathways provided by the expertise of partner organisations, we aim to create opportunities that elevate Indian footballers to global platforms.

Strategic International Pathways

India Khelo Football along with our International Pathway provider offers two distinct international pathways for aspiring footballers, ensuring every player has access to opportunities tailored to their unique aspirations.

Unleashing India’s Football Talent

  • IKF believes in harnessing India’s abundant football resources.
  • By integrating grassroots scouting with structured international pathways through our partners, we ensure that talented players have access to comprehensive career development systems.

A Global Pathway for Indian Footballers

  • India Khelo Football invites you to join us in this transformative journey of empowering young athletes with unparalleled opportunities.
  • Whether pursuing education in the U.S. or competing professionally in Europe, our mission is clear: placing Indian footballers on the global map through structured and transparent pathways.
  • Let’s elevate Indian football together!

Commitment to Global Exposure

  • Global exposure is indispensable for advancing Indian football.
  • Players gain access to tailored opportunities—whether academic or professional—based on their aspirations.
  • Training programs meet international standards, preparing players for success on global platforms.
