IMT Ghaziabad (IKF-CoE) Program

Institute of Management Technology, Ghaziabad (IMT), and India Khelo Football (IKF) have joined forces to unlock the potential of Indian football through data-driven insights.
A Groundbreaking Collaboration
This unique partnership brings together IMT's renowned research capabilities with IKF's extensive on-the-ground experience in football development. While IKF is busy conducting grassroots trials, workshops, and collecting valuable data, IMT's Sports Research Centre, led by Dr. Kanishka Pandey , is transforming that data into actionable insights.

Visit Website

What this means for indian football

This collaborative approach promises to be a game-changer for Indian football. By combining practical experience with rigorous research, we can expect:

  • More effective talent identification and development programs
  • Evidence-based strategies for improving coaching and training
  • Greater understanding of the factors influencing football participation
  • A clearer roadmap for the future of Indian football

A Shared Vision for Success

IMT and IKF share a deep commitment to elevating Indian football on the global stage. This partnership is a testament to that commitment, and we are enthusiastic about the potential it holds for transforming the landscape of Indian football.
