India Khelo Football (IKF)

is a Section-8 not-for-profit organisation building a transparent and standardised grassroots talent identification system for Indian football.

IKF is not a club, not an academy, and not an agent.

It is a neutral platform designed to ensure that talent is discovered, evaluated, and connected to opportunity.

Footprint Projects

These footprint Projects are special initiatives that brings together the finest talent, top scouts, global opportunities and IKF Pathway 360 under one roof. These projects are designed to bring spotlight on rising stars and propel them toward professional football pathways and at the same time focuses on community development with football as enabler
