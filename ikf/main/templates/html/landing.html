Become an IKF Regional Partner

Who Can Become a Regional Execution Partner?

We collaborate with partners who share a similar vision of developing the Indian football grassroots system.

  • Have your own football ground for hosting trials and training.
  • Run a football academy or be actively engaged with the footballing community.
  • Have a minimum of 3+ years of experience in football development.
  • Have an operational team of at least 5–6 members.
  • Be committed to long–term player development and community engagement.

If you meet these criteria and want to grow football in your region, IKF is your ideal partner.

Why Partner with IKF?

With a presence in over 125 cities, IKF is India's leading grassroots football platform—offering structured trials, talent scouting, and the IKF Pathway 360 program. As a Regional Partner, you become part of a national movement that is changing how football dreams are nurtured in India.

  • Access to structured trial formats, scouting frameworks and central support.
  • Integration into IKF's national communication, branding, and marketing channels.
  • Support in planning, executing and reviewing football activities in your region.

Role & Benefits of a Regional Partner

As an IKF Regional Execution Partner, you play a crucial role in making football accessible to every aspiring player in your region.

  • Host IKF Football Trials in your region.
  • Help talented players access real football career pathways with IKF Pathway 360.
  • Build strong local football ecosystems with schools, academies and communities.
  • Get access to exclusive IKF resources, playbooks and operational support.
  • Collaborate with IKF's central team for seamless execution of football activities.
  • Strengthen your own brand as a trusted football institution in your region.
