MyFirstKick x India Khelo Football

Powering India’s Largest Grassroots Scouting Movement

The Digital Engine Behind IKF’s Scouting & Showcasing

India Khelo Football’s nationwide talent discovery ecosystem is built on a strong digital backbone — MyFirstKick (MFK).

MFK provides IKF with a robust, endtoend platform to manage every stage of scouting and showcasing, ensuring transparency, efficiency, and scale across India.

From player registration to profile creation, from rating systems to showcasing talent to clubs — everything flows seamlessly through MyFirstKick.

Powering Asia’s Largest Talent Hunt

India Khelo Football’s scouting program is now recognized as Asia’s largest grassroots talent hunt.

MyFirstKick plays a pivotal role by:

  • Managing registrations at scale
  • Digitizing the entire scouting workflow
  • Ensuring transparent, datadriven player ratings
  • Showcasing top performers to clubs worldwide

Together, IKF and MFK are building a future where every talented child — from any corner of India — gets a fair chance to be discovered.

A LinkedIn for Footballers

MyFirstKick functions as India’s first professional networking platform dedicated to footballers.

It enables players to:

  • Register and build verified football profiles
  • Upload performance data, videos, and achievements
  • Receive objective ratings from IKF scouts
  • Get showcased directly to clubs and academies

For players, it’s a digital identity.

For clubs, it’s a trusted talent database.

For Indian football, it’s a gamechanging ecosystem.

Helping Clubs Discover Talent Faster & Smarter

Scouting undiscovered grassroots talent is timeconsuming and expensive.

MFK solves this by giving clubs and academies:

  • Access to thousands of verified players
  • A first level of evaluation through IKF’s rating system
  • Tools to shortlist, filter, and track players
  • Significant savings in time, effort, and scouting costs

Clubs simply log in, explore talent, and identify prospects — all before stepping onto the field.

50,000+ Players. Growing Every Day.

MyFirstKick is one of India’s largest digital football talent pools with:

  • 50,000+ registered players
  • Multiple professional clubs using the Club Login
  • Verified player data from IKF’s nationwide scouting network

This scale allows clubs to discover talent from regions they’ve never reached before.

Trusted by International Academies

Beyond India, MyFirstKick is also used by international academies looking to handpick emerging talent from the country.

The platform gives them structured, reliable, and comparable data — something grassroots football has lacked for decades.
