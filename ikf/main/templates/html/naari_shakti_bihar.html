IKF Naari Shakti – Bihar Edition

For The Girls. By The Girls. Of The Girls.

supported and operated by AtulyaAnant Foundation & Kridanta

Why Bihar?

Across Bihar, thousands of girls dream of playing football, yet many face barriers such as limited access to training, lack of awareness, social constraints, and the absence of structured pathways.

IKF Naari Shakti is changing that.

Through football, mentorship, health awareness, and career guidance, Naari Shakti creates a safe and empowering environment where girls can develop both on and off the field.

It's More Than Football

IKF Naari Shakti uses football as a tool to build:

  • Confidence
  • Leadership
  • Teamwork
  • Resilience
  • Health Awareness
  • Aspirational Thinking

The objective is not only to identify football talent but to empower young girls to become future leaders within their communities.

What happens at IKF Naari Shakti?

Naari Shakti is IKF's flagship girls-first initiative designed to increase participation, create opportunities, and build long-term pathways for girls in football.

The program combines:

Talent Discovery

Providing girls with a platform to showcase their football abilities.

Football Development

Access to structured football activities and coaching through regional partners.

Health & Wellbeing

Sessions focused on menstrual health, athlete wellness, nutrition, and injury prevention.

Parent Support

Helping families understand the opportunities available through sport.

Career Pathways

Introducing girls to opportunities both within and beyond football.

National Platform

Connecting talented players to broader opportunities through the IKF ecosystem.

Why Your Support Matters

The future of girls' participation in sports cannot be built by sporting organizations alone.

It requires visionary partners who believe that access, opportunity, and empowerment should not be determined by geography, gender, or economic background.

By supporting IKF Naari Shakti, you are helping create:

Opportunities Where None Exist

Many girls participating in Naari Shakti experience their first structured football program through the initiative.

Confidence Through Sport

Football becomes a tool for building self-belief, leadership, teamwork, and resilience.

Health & Wellbeing Awareness

Girls receive critical education regarding menstrual health, nutrition, injury prevention, and athlete wellbeing.

Stronger Family Support Systems

Parents become active partners in encouraging and sustaining girls' participation in sports.

Pathways Beyond Participation

The initiative introduces girls to opportunities both on and off the field, including coaching, refereeing, sports management, and athlete development pathways.

Sustainable Community Impact

Every activation creates awareness among schools, communities, coaches, and local stakeholders, leaving a lasting impact beyond the event itself.

Impact Beyond the Field

Project IKF Naari Shakti contributes directly to the following SDGs:

  • Gender Equality
  • Quality Education
  • Good Health & Wellbeing
  • Reduced Inequalities
  • Community Development

Every girl who steps onto the field gains more than a sporting opportunity — she gains confidence, visibility, support, and the belief that her aspirations matter.
