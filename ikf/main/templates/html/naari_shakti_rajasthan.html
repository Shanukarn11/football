IKF Naari Shakti – Rajasthan

For The Girls. By The Girls. Of The Girls.

Empowering Rajasthan's Next Generation Through Football. Across Rajasthan, talented girls are breaking barriers and redefining what is possible through sport. IKF Naari Shakti is creating opportunities for girls from cities, towns, and rural communities by providing access to football, mentorship, health education, and long-term development pathways.

What is Naari Shakti?

Naari Shakti is a girls-first football initiative by India Khelo Football (IKF) that combines talent discovery with holistic development.

The initiative creates safe and inclusive football environments where girls can participate, learn, and thrive.

The program focuses on:

  • Football Talent Identification
  • Football Clinics & Skill Development
  • Menstrual Health Awareness
  • Parent Engagement
  • Life Skills & Leadership Development
  • Career Exploration & Employability
  • National Football Opportunities

Why Rajasthan?

Rajasthan has immense football potential and a growing community of young girls eager to participate in sport.

Naari Shakti works alongside local football stakeholders, schools, and community partners to ensure that girls receive equal opportunities to develop their talent and pursue their ambitions.

More Than a Football Program

Naari Shakti is designed to create impact far beyond the pitch.

Through structured workshops and football activities, girls gain:

  • Confidence and Self-Belief
  • Leadership Skills
  • Discipline and Teamwork
  • Health & Wellness Awareness
  • Communication Skills
  • Exposure to New Opportunities
