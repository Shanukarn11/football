For Parents

IKF Journey

IKF is a non-profit initiative with no revenue model. Everything we do is driven by one belief — building a strong, inclusive football ecosystem where every player gets a fair chance to grow and stay in the game.

We are not just running programs; we are creating pathways. Pathways for players to discover football, to continue playing, and to access opportunities that otherwise may not exist.

The Challenge

Why This Matters

Many players start their journey — but very few are able to stay in the ecosystem due to lack of support, structure, or resources.

IKF is working to change that. We are ensuring that:

Players don't drop off after initial exposure

Structured programs and consistent touchpoints keep players engaged beyond their first trial.

Opportunities are consistently available

Regular trials, pathways and mentorship ensure there is always a next step for every player.

The ecosystem continues to grow stronger and more inclusive

Every season, IKF expands reach — from 17 cities to 135+ — ensuring no talent goes unseen.
