The Purpose

  • At India Khelo Football (IKF), we've seen your passion, discipline, and dedication on the field. Now, IKF wants to stand beside you off the field too — helping you build a stable and meaningful career without losing your football identity.
  • The IKF Pathway 360 – Job/Apprenticeship Program ensures that players like you, who've been part of the IKF ecosystem, always have a strong support system — one that helps you grow professionally while staying connected to your football dreams.
  • Stay in the game, and keep inspiring others to stay in the game — IKF has got you covered.

What Makes This Special

  • IKF is not a recruitment company, nor do we charge players or corporates. Through the IKF Pathway 360 Network, several corporate partners have come forward to offer job opportunities and apprenticeships to disciplined and passionate footballers — to support their journey off the field as recommended by IKF.
  • Whether you are just a class 10 pass-out or an engineer or doctor; 18 years old or 30 years old, you are eligible if you've shown commitment and love for football. Your passion will have a parachute through IKF — a support system to help you land on your feet and continue your journey forward.
  • IKF will first evaluate each candidate through a 1-week evaluation program, and only then recommend them to corporates best suited to their strengths.

What You'll Get

Job opportunities in Marketing, Sales, Operations, Communications, Front Office, Security and many more.

A 1-week evaluation program before recommendation to corporates.

Starting salary from ₹20,000 per month.

Continued mentorship through IKF's 360 ecosystem.

Direct corporate recommendations based on your evaluation and performance.

Who Can Apply

  • Any player who has been part of the IKF ecosystem or trials.
  • Footballers, regardless of qualification or age, who are ready to build a career while staying connected to football.

How It Works

  1. Fill the Application Form using the link provided.
  2. Join the 1-Week Evaluation Program conducted by IKF.
  3. Get Recommended to Corporates through IKF's 360 Network.
  4. Start Your Professional Journey with confidence and support.
