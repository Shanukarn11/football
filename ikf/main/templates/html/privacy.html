Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how India Khelo Football (IKF) collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our website, services, or features (including login or posting integrations).

Last updated: {{ "2025-12-26" }}

1) What data we collect

Depending on what you use on IKF, we may collect:

  • Basic identity data: name, email address, phone number (if provided)
  • Profile / social data: LinkedIn profile ID / public profile URL (if you connect LinkedIn)
  • Account and usage data: login timestamps, pages/actions used on the site
  • Technical data: device/browser info, IP address (for security and analytics)

2) Why we collect it

  • To enable login and account access
  • To support posting / integrations (e.g., LinkedIn-related features if enabled)
  • To operate, improve, and secure IKF services (fraud prevention, debugging, performance)
  • To contact you for support or important service updates (when required)

3) Where your data is stored

Your information is stored in IKF’s internal systems, including our database (DB) and supporting infrastructure used to run the website and its services.

4) Who can access it

Access is restricted to authorized IKF admins and team members who need it to operate, maintain, and support the platform.

5) Data deletion requests

You can request deletion of your data by emailing us from your registered email address. We may verify your request before processing it.

Email: support@indiakhelofootball.com

6) Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact: support@indiakhelofootball.com

Note: If IKF adds new features (e.g., additional integrations), we may update this policy to reflect changes in data handling.
