Scout on Wheels

In every lane, kids chase a ball with dreams in their eyes. But too often, those dreams fade without guidance, opportunity, or the right platform.

India Khelo Football (IKF) is changing that. With Scout on Wheels, we took football directly to communities— schools, villages, and towns—so that every child, boy or girl, can "stay in the game" and find a pathway in football.

Scout on Wheels focuses on

Community Empowerment

1. Football as a tool for growth and opportunity.
2. Community mobilisation through grassroots clinics and open trials.
3. Stronger community participation beyond the pitch.

Girls in the Game

1. Dedicated clinics and clear pathways for young girls.
2. Inclusive access at every level of participation.
3. Long-term focus on gender equity in sport.

Pathway 360 Safety Net

1. Pathways beyond playing.
2. Opportunities across coaching, analysis, sports science, and operations.
3. Sustainable careers and life skills in sport.

Equal Access

1. Talent as the only currency.
2. Every player and trial is documented.
3. Transparent, merit-based evaluation.
4. Impact tracking through data-led systems.
