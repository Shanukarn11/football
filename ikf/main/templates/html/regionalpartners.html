Who Can Become a Regional Execution Partner?

We collaborate with partners who share similar vision of developing Indian Football Grassroots system.

  • Have your own football ground for hosting trials and training
  • Run a football academy or be actively engaged with the footballing community
  • Have a minimum of 3+ years of experience in football development
  • Have an operational team of at least 5–6 members
  • Have a minimum of 3+ years of experience in football development

If you meet these criteria and want to grow football in your region, IKF is your ideal partner.

Why Partner with IKF?

With a presence in over 125 cities, IKF is India’s leading grassroots football platform, offering structured trials, talent scouting, and the revolutionary IKF Pathway 360 program. As a Regional Partner, you become part of a national movement that is changing how football dreams are nurtured in India.

Role & Benefits of a Regional Partner

As an IKF Regional Execution Partner, you will:

  • Host IKF Football Trials in your region
  • Help talented players from your region access real football career pathways with IKF Pathway 360
  • Get access to exclusive IKF resources and support
  • Collaborate with IKF’s central team for seamless execution for football-related activities to grow your brand.
