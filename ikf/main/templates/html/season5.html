Important Participation Guidelines

Fees & Eligibility

  • A one-time registration fee applies only at City Trials; all subsequent rounds are free. No fee for girls and underprivileged boys.
  • Girls born 2009–2016 and boys born 2009–2016 are eligible.

Refund Policy

  • Fees are refundable only if IKF cancels a City Trial.
  • No refunds for personal no-shows or schedule conflicts.

Fair Play & Conduct

  • Selection is purely talent-based — no quotas or limits.
  • Any malpractice (e.g., false IDs) leads to immediate disqualification and a 3-year ban.
  • You may be asked to play in both primary and secondary positions.

Logistics & Support

  • Participants arrange their own travel, accommodation, and meals for each venue.
  • Trial dates account for local conditions but may shift due to unforeseen issues.
  • We'll notify you in advance of any changes via email and SMS.

Fee Waiver for Underprivileged Children

At IKF, we believe football should be accessible to everyone. Boys from economically underprivileged backgrounds can participate free of cost.

To ensure eligibility, any one of the following government-issued documents is required:

BPL / Antyodaya Card
Income Certificate
EWS Certificate
