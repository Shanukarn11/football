About IKF Trials

India Khelo Football (IKF) bridges the gap between grassroots passion and professional opportunity through its non-profit, data-driven National Trials. Guided by the vision "Aap Khelo, Mauka Hum Denge!", IKF provides a fair and transparent platform where players aged 10–17 (boys and girls) can showcase their talent in front of certified scouts and professional clubs — without geographical or financial barriers.

IKF is committed to creating equal opportunities in football. Participation in trials remains free for girls, ensuring wider access and encouraging greater female participation in the sport. This season, IKF introduces Project IKF Naari Shakti, a dedicated initiative featuring all-girls trials designed to empower young female footballers and create stronger pathways for their growth across India.
