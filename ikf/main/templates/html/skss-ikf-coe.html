SKSS (IKF-CoE) Program

Welcome to the IKF Centre of Excellence at Hajipur!
In collaboration with SKSSSS and Ansha Football Academy, we are proud to launch a premier training hub integrating elite football training with educational empowerment and community development.

Our Programs

We offer tailored training programs for various age groups, emphasizing skill development, physical fitness, and tactical knowledge. Special initiatives focus on empowering young women and providing opportunities for underprivileged athletes.

Facilities

Our state-of-the-art facilities include professional-grade football pitches, educational classrooms, and residential accommodations designed to foster growth both on and off the field.

Joining the CoE

Interested in joining? Check out our detailed application process and eligibility criteria. We provide scholarships and financial aid to eligible candidates to ensure that every talented athlete has the chance to excel.

Community Impact

We are committed to using football as a tool for social change. Our programs aim to improve life skills, promote gender equality, and boost community health and well-being.
