Big News: SpotOnn & India Khelo Football Collaborate with Tyger Capital to Revolutionize Football in Rajasthan!

We are beyond thrilled to announce a dynamic partnership between SpotOnn, a leader in sports coaching and management, and India Khelo Football (IKF)! This collaboration is set to transform the football scene in Rajasthan by identifying and nurturing undiscovered talent across urban and rural areas.

About India Khelo Football (IKF): IKF is revolutionizing Indian football by connecting grassroots talent directly with professional leagues. Dedicated to democratizing access to the sport, IKF is committed to giving every aspiring footballer a fair shot at success.

About SpotOnn: As India's first sports management firm to integrate IoT technology with sports coaching, SpotOnn transforms athlete training and performance with data-driven insights. SpotOnn is dedicated to playing sports a staple of daily life for the youth, promoting sustained engagement and development.

About Tyger Capital: Tyger Capital specializes in providing accessible financial solutions, particularly focusing on empowering entrepreneurs and homeowners in semi-urban and rural areas. Their commitment to simplifying financing processes aligns with regional and national economic growth initiatives.

Tyger IKF Trials Take Over Rajasthan: Get ready, because the Tyger IKF trials are expanding to electrifying cities— Jaipur, Udaipur, Ajmer, Jodhpur, Kota, Sikar, Bikaner, and Sri Ganganagar—and vibrant villages— Dudu, Banswara, Tonk, Pali, Nagaur, Bundi, Nokha, Jhunjhunnu and Hanumangarh. This expansion is not just a step; it's a giant leap for football enthusiasts in Rajasthan!

Empowering Rural Talent: We believe every aspiring footballer deserves a shot at glory, regardless of their financial background. That’s why we’re rolling out subsidized rates for our village venues, and in some special cases, we’re offering free participation. Because where there’s talent, there should be no barriers!
