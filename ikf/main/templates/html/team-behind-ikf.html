About Us

India Khelo Football (IKF) is a non-profit initiative dedicated to transforming the landscape of Indian football. We are not a club, academy, or agent; instead, we serve as a platform that democratizes football by connecting grassroots talent with professional opportunities.

Our mission is to discover and nurture football talent across India, providing structured career paths that integrate educational and professional opportunities.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To discover and nurture football talent across India, providing structured career paths that integrate educational and professional opportunities. We aim to equip players with the skills needed for both athletic and personal success, ensuring they can contribute positively to their communities.

Our Vision

We envision India as a leading nation in global football, recognized for its comprehensive development of athletes who excel in both sports and academics. Our goal is to transform the landscape of Indian football by making it accessible and inclusive, using the sport as a powerful tool for societal change and empowerment.

Modern Platform

IKF stands as a platform that blends physical trials with digital technology to scout and connect amateur football players with global opportunities. This approach ensures that talent from even the most remote areas can be discovered and nurtured.

Holistic Development

Beyond athletic prowess, we focus on the holistic development of our players. This includes academic excellence, community engagement, and inclusivity. We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who can excel both on and off the field.

Global Collaborations
