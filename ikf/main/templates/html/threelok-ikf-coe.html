In collaboration with 3lok Football Fitness Hub, IKF is thrilled to launch the Centre of Excellence at the vibrant location in Bengaluru. This partnership aims to fuse 3lok Football Fitness Hub’s state of art facility with IKF’s proven football trials and International Partner Workshops.
