Football for the Goals

India Khelo Football joins the United Nations initiative

Officially Recognized as a Member of UN Football for the Goals

India Khelo Football (IKF) is proud to be officially recognized as a member of the United Nations Football for the Goals initiative, a global platform that brings together football stakeholders to advance the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

This recognition reflects IKF's commitment to using football as a powerful tool for social impact by creating opportunities for young players, promoting equality, and strengthening grassroots football development across India.

About Football for the Goals

Football for the Goals is a United Nations initiative designed to harness the global influence of football to support sustainable development.

The initiative encourages football organizations, clubs, academies, leagues, and stakeholders to integrate sustainability and social responsibility into their activities while promoting awareness of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

By joining this global movement, India Khelo Football becomes part of a network of organizations committed to creating positive change through the power of sport.

IKF's Commitment

As an official member of Football for the Goals, India Khelo Football aligns its programs and activities with the values promoted by the United Nations initiative.

IKF continues to work towards:

Expanding Grassroots Opportunities

Providing players from across India with access to national trials and professional football pathways.

Promoting Gender Equality

Encouraging participation of girls in football and creating inclusive opportunities within the sport.

Youth Development Through Sport

Using football as a platform to develop discipline, teamwork, leadership and confidence among young athletes.

Promoting Fair Play and Inclusion

Building a football ecosystem that values diversity, respect and equal opportunity.

IKF Supporting the Sustainable Development Goals

Through its initiatives and programs, India Khelo Football actively contributes to several United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

SDG 3 – Good Health and Well-being

Football promotes active lifestyles and improves physical and mental health among young players.

SDG 4 – Quality Education

Sports provide learning opportunities that develop discipline, teamwork and leadership skills.

SDG 5 – Gender Equality

IKF encourages girls' participation in football by creating equal opportunities in trials and programs.

SDG 10 – Reduced Inequalities

By conducting trials across different cities and regions, IKF ensures opportunities are accessible to players from diverse backgrounds.

SDG 17 – Partnerships for the Goals

Through collaborations with organizations, academies and football stakeholders, IKF strengthens partnerships that help expand opportunities in football.
