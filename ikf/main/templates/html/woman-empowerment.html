Empowering Her Through Football

In a country where societal norms often confine girls to traditional roles, football is becoming a tool of freedom. At India Khelo Football (IKF), we believe that every girl deserves a chance to lead — not just in sport, but in life.

Through structured programs, nationwide trials, and mentorship, we're using the beautiful game to empower girls across India — one kick at a time.

Anaya's Goal: Empowering Underprivileged Girls Through Football

Anaya Sehgal, a 17-year-old midfielder in the Singapore Women's Premier League (WPL), is using her goals to ignite hope and opportunity for girls in India who lack basic equipment.

  • Proven Performer: In 2024 Anaya scored 14 goals for BG Tampines Rovers and her high school team and raised Rs 1 Lac. In 2025 she played for Lion City Sailors in the WPL and her high school team and scored 16 goals.
